Random Movies I've Watched in the Last Few Years

My Top Three

1. Se7en

I just love the way the suspense of the film mixes with the mystery up until the very end.

2. Jagt

A deep and moving Danish drama that made me think of how fragile human reputation can be.

3. Yes Man

A cute and funny rom-com with one of my all time favorite male actors and an interesting and rather strong philosophical idea.
